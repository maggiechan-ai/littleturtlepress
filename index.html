How It Works

Three simple steps that unlock deeper learning through multimodal play.

📖
Step 1

Read the Book

15 stunning wildlife photographs with simple vocabulary, fun facts, and parent reading prompts based on the PEER method.

🦁
Step 2

Hold the Figurine

Pair with any animal figurine (Schleich, Safari Ltd, Papo) so your child can touch, feel, and connect with what they see.

🧠
Step 3

Learn New Words

Multi-sensory engagement — seeing, hearing, and holding — builds 40% stronger vocabulary retention than reading alone.
