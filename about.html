Slow, Steady, and Wise

We believe the best learning happens at a child's pace — not a screen's speed. That's why we named ourselves after the turtle.

Our Story

Why a Turtle?

In a world that moves fast, we believe young children need something different. The turtle carries its home on its back — a symbol of wisdom, patience, and steady growth.

Little Turtle Press was born from a simple observation: toddlers learn best when they can see something real, hear about it from someone they love, and hold it in their hands. Not through flashy apps. Not through screens. Through the timeless combination of books, play, and connection.

Every parent has watched their child light up when they recognize an animal from a book in real life. We wanted to make that magic happen more often — by creating books with real wildlife photography that children can match to the figurines in their toy basket.

🐢
Our Mission

"To create the world's best multimodal learning experience for little explorers."

We're not trying to replace screen time with guilt. We're building something so compelling, so engaging, and so effective that families naturally reach for our books instead.

Our Method: Four Pillars of Learning

Every book we create is built on four research-backed principles.

📸

Real Photography

Professional wildlife images — not cartoons. Research shows toddlers transfer learning from real photos to real life more effectively.

🧸

Figurine Pairing

Every book is designed to work with popular animal figurines, adding a tactile dimension that deepens learning and memory.

💬

PEER Method

Parent reading prompts based on the proven PEER technique: Prompt, Evaluate, Expand, Repeat — making every page a conversation.

👨‍👩‍👧

Parent Guides

Built-in notes help caregivers maximize learning without needing a background in child development. Every parent becomes a great teacher.

What Makes Us Different

We're not just making picture books — we're building a learning system.

🔬

Research-First Design

Every design decision — from using real photos to including parent prompts — is grounded in published child development research. We don't guess; we cite.

🌍

National Geographic Energy

Multi-Sensory by Design

See it in the book. Hear about it from a parent. Hold it in your hands. Three senses working together build neural pathways that one alone never could.

📖

Screen-Free Philosophy

No QR codes. No app tie-ins. No digital upsells. Just books, figurines, and human connection — the way learning has worked for thousands of years.
